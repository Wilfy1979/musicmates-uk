MusicMates — Coming soon

MusicMates will be a UK-focused network to match fans by bands, coordinate meetups at gigs, and build micro-communities around local music scenes. Features planned: band-based matching, gig calendar aggregation, event-based ephemeral chat, and artist-curated listings. The goal is to make it easy for people who love the same bands to meet, collaborate, and attend gigs together safely.

Proof workflow

Instead of reading about peoples mental health or who's been recently arrested, MusicMates promises to be positive, match with another music lover and check the latest built in gig guide and your both on your way.
